App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS, you may be asked to allow tracking for personalized ads. You can change this choice anytime in Settings → Privacy → Tracking.

Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

Data Retention

Local preferences remain on your device until you uninstall the App or clear app data. Ad and analytics partners retain data per their own policies.

Your Choices
